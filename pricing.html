Good to know

Pricing, minus the small print

Community rate

Full-time students, seniors 65+ and those between jobs practice unlimited for $89/month. Just ask at the desk — no proof theatre, we trust you.

Pause anytime

Travel, injury, new baby, life — freeze your membership for up to two months a year from your phone in about thirty seconds.

Class credit promise

Cancel a booking up to four hours before class and the credit returns to your account automatically. Late cancels due to emergencies? Just talk to us.

Questions

Asked at the front desk this month

I'm brand new. Which option should I start with?

Start with your free local class, then take the new-member month at $59. Fourteen classes in your first month is the sweet spot most beginners land on — you'll know quickly if unlimited suits you.

Can I share a class pack?

Yes — the 10-class pack can be shared with one other person. Both of you just need an account so we know who's in the room. Memberships are individual, but the monthly guest pass helps.

Do I need my own mat?

No. Every pricing option includes mats, blocks, straps, bolsters, blankets and towels, plus tea in the lounge after class. Bring a water bottle if you like — we have a refill station.

What if money is tight right now?

Come anyway. Beyond the community rate, we keep a quiet pay-what-you-can fund sponsored by members, and a work-trade program (four hours at the desk per month) for unlimited practice. Email us — it's never a big deal.

Is there a contract for unlimited?

No contracts. It's a rolling monthly membership you can cancel or pause anytime from your account. We'd rather earn your renewal than lock you into it.
