Classes

Twelve formats. One promise: meet you where you are.

Every class lists its level, length and energy so you can choose with confidence. When in doubt, start with Foundations or any class marked "all levels."

Vinyasa Flow

Our signature breath-led practice. Continuous, creative sequences build heat and strength, then melt into a long savasana. Options offered throughout.

  • All levels
  • 60 min
  • Energising

Slow Flow

Half the pace, twice the attention. A gentler vinyasa with more time in each shape — ideal for tired bodies, injuries in recovery, or quieter days.

  • All levels
  • 60 min
  • Grounding

Power Yoga

A strong, athletic practice with core work, arm balances and longer holds. Come ready to sweat; leave pleasantly wrung out.

  • Intermediate
  • 75 min
  • Strong

Hatha

Classic postures held and explored one at a time, with clear alignment cues and breath woven through. The roots of everything we teach.

  • All levels
  • 75 min
  • Steady

Restorative

Candlelight, blankets and bolsters. Fully supported poses held for five to ten minutes each, designed to genuinely downshift your nervous system.

  • All levels
  • 75 min
  • Deep rest

Yin Yoga

Long, floor-based holds that work into joints and connective tissue. Quiet music, minimal talking, maximum stillness.

  • All levels
  • 75 min
  • Meditative

Foundations

A friendly on-ramp for absolute beginners and returning practitioners. Poses, props, breathing and studio etiquette — questions very welcome.

  • Beginners
  • 60 min
  • Supportive

Mat Pilates

Precise, low-impact core conditioning on the mat. Builds the deep stability that makes every other class — and daily life — feel easier.

  • All levels
  • 45 min
  • Core

Breathwork & Meditation

Thirty minutes of guided breathing, thirty of seated practice. No experience needed — cushions, chairs and complete beginners all welcome.

  • All levels
  • 60 min
  • Stillness

Sunrise Flow

A 6:30am wake-up call your body will thank you for. Forty-five energising minutes, then tea on the house before your day begins.

  • All levels
  • 45 min
  • Early

Prenatal

Safe, strengthening movement for every trimester, led by certified prenatal teachers. A warm community of parents-to-be included.

  • All trimesters
  • 60 min
  • Nurturing

Gentle & Chair Yoga

Accessible movement with chair support for seniors, limited mobility, or anyone rebuilding after injury. Steady, social and genuinely joyful.

  • Seniors 60+
  • 45 min
  • Accessible
